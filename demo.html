Key Research Insights

Quantum Achievement: The Variational Quantum Classifier (VQC) achieved 80% accuracy, matching Random Forest performance and demonstrating competitive quantum ML capabilities on classical hardware simulation.

Quantum Configuration: EfficientSU2 ansatz with 2 repetitions, 2-layer feature map, optimized over 50 iterations using COBYLA optimizer.

Production Readiness: SVM leads at 90% accuracy with fast inference (<5ms), making it ideal for production deployment. Quantum models show promise but require hardware acceleration for practical deployment.
